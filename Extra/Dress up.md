### Core jacket terms (super common)

- **Collar** – fabric around the neck
- **Neckline** – the shape/opening around the neck
- **Shoulder seam** – where sleeve meets the body
- **Chest** – width around the upper body
- **Waist** – midsection of the jacket
- **Hem / Bottom hem** – lower edge of the jacket
- **Cuff** – end of the sleeve
### Sleeve-related

- **Sleeve length** – shoulder to cuff
- **Armhole** – opening where sleeve attaches
- **Placket (sleeve)** – opening with buttons/snaps (less common on jackets)

### Front & closure

- **Placket** – fabric strip that holds buttons or snaps
- **Zipper tape** – fabric on either side of the zipper
- **Storm flap** – flap covering zipper to block wind/rain
- **Chin guard** – soft cover at top of zipper
### Fit & structure

- **Yoke** – panel across upper back or shoulders
- **Side seams** – vertical seams on the sides
- **Vent** – slit at the back or sides for movement
- **Drop shoulder** – relaxed shoulder seam below the natural shoulder
### Functional details

- **Drawcord** – adjustable string (usually at hem or hood)
- **Toggle** – plastic/metal stopper on drawcord
- **Pocket welt** – reinforced pocket opening
- **Hand-warmer pockets** – pockets placed higher for comfort
### Hood (if present)

- **Hood brim** – small edge extending from hood
- **Adjuster** – cord or tab to tighten hood
### Lining & insulation

- **Lining** – inner fabric
- **Shell** – outer fabric
- **Insulation / Filling** – material for warmth (down, polyester, etc.)
